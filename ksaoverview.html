Korean Student Association

Overview:

The purpose of the KSA is to promote the heritage, identity, and pride of Korean and Korean-Americans here on campus; to foster mutual understanding and friendship between Koreans, Korean-Americans, and those persons of other nationalities; and to preserve Korean and Korean-American students’ rights and interests.

Quotes from Board Members:

“Three fold: create community that embodies both Korean-Americans and people interested in Korean culture, promote Korean culture, third, combination of both” Ben Kim, KSA President 2018-2019
“To bring all the Korean-Americans the university of michigan together, one centralized group” Yujin Kim, KSA Membership Chair 2018-2019
