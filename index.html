KSA x TASA

Presence + Impact on Campus - AMCULT 352 Term Project by Elena Fang and Inwoo Cho

Overview & Objective:

Our objective is to analyze the impact of Korean Student Association and Taiwanese American Student Association on the University of Michigan campus. Ethnicity is a huge part of what people tie to their identity and being able to spread culture has largely been an objective of both clubs of KSA and TASA. Throughout this website, we'll be analyzing several aspects of both organizations on campus to see how well they reflect each respective culture and how well they have accomplished their prospective missions. Scroll down to get a bigger overview of our objectives and navigate through the links on the left to read our analysis.
